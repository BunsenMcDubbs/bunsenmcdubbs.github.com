About

I am passionate about computer science, robotics and photography.

I am currently at freshman at Georgia Tech studying Computer Science. Linux/Unix shell is my comfort zone since I alternate between a MacBook Air and an aging Ubuntu desktop.

I have a broad interest in the field of computer science and technology in general. Starting with Java learned in school, I've branched out and dabbled in all sorts of languages and technologies. In order of familiarity, I know Java, Javascript, Python and I'm Haskell-curious.

I've recently started learning about backend web development and working on the full stack with nodejs. Angular.js and Node.js are currently on my plate rounding out experience in web development and getting a little bit of everything. My previous work on the BeagleCar had also led me to dive into much nitty-grittier details of computing. Next (eventually/distant future) on my list is a dive even deeper and playing with C/Assembly to figure out what really goes on in the computer (or at least a little glimpse).
